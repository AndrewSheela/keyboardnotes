Log Files

JGeneration follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected includes IP addresses, browser type, ISP, date/time stamps, and referring/exit pages. This data is used for analyzing trends and administering the site, and is not linked to personally identifiable information.
