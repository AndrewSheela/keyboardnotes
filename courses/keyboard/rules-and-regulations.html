📜 Rules & Regulations

  3. ⏰If you cannot join the class, you must provide a valid reason before the class begins. Otherwise, it will be considered absent.
  4. 📝Attendance Rule: Type your name and roll number in the comment section before 8:50 PM or at the class ending time.

  12. 📣We will not send any private messages. All communication will be shared in the group and during the live Google Meet class. Stay active and read all messages posted by Admin: Brother Andrew & Brother Elijah Ruben.
