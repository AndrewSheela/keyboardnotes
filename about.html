About JGeneration

Empowering worship through music. Providing free keyboard notes, lyrics, and chords for the global Christian community.

Our Mission

At JGeneration Ministry, we believe that music is a powerful tool for worship and spiritual growth. Our mission is to bridge the gap for aspiring musicians and worship leaders by providing high-quality, easy-to-read keyboard notes and accurate lyrics for both contemporary and traditional Christian songs.

We aim to remove technical barriers so that your focus remains entirely on glorifying God through your talents.
